Welcome to Spaceman!

How to Play:

  1. Select one letter at a time.
  2. If the letter is correct, it will appear in the corresponding space.
  3. If the letter is incorrect, the Spaceman will begin to appear.
  4. You will have a total of 6 tries to correctly guess the word.
